How to play

  1. Player A writes 5 things.
  2. Player A arranges them in the order they think Player B fucks with.
  3. Player B arranges the same 5 things in their real order.
  4. A score is shown based on matching positions.
  5. Roles switch and the game repeats.
  6. Final scores are compared.
